Hunta

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 8 July 2026

Hunta is a private hunting portfolio app. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, where it is stored, and the control you have over it. The short version: your records belong to you, they are private by default, and we never sell your data or use it for advertising.

Who we are

Hunta is operated by Armindo Manuel Bento (“we”, “us”). For anything in this policy, contact armindo1706@gmail.com.

Data we collect

  • Account data — your name, email address, and a user identifier, received from Google or Apple when you sign in. If you use “Hide My Email” with Apple, we only see the relay address.
  • Hunt records — the content you create: species, dates and times, weapon and ammunition details, and free-text descriptions.
  • Location data — the precise location of a hunt, captured from your device’s GPS or entered by you, stored as part of the hunt record.
  • Photos and videos — media you attach to hunt records from your camera or photo library.
  • Route files — GPX tracks you import (e.g. from Garmin or Strava), stored verbatim, plus distance and duration derived from them.

We do not collect advertising identifiers, we do not track you across other apps or websites, and we do not use third-party analytics.

How we use it

Solely to provide the app: authenticate you, store your portfolio, display your records, and render your routes on the map. Nothing else.

Where it is stored

Your data is stored with Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Cloud Storage), operated by Google LLC, which acts as our data processor. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and protected by per-user security rules: every record is tied to your account, and no other user can read or modify it.

Privacy of your records

Your portfolio is private by default. Only you can see your hunt records, locations, photos, and routes. If the app offers a feature to publish a hunt publicly, sharing is always an explicit action you take per record — nothing is made public automatically.

Data retention and deletion

Your records are kept for as long as you keep your account. You can delete individual records and their media at any time in the app. You can also delete your entire account, which permanently removes your hunt records, photos, videos, route files, and account data. To request deletion by email instead, write to armindo1706@gmail.com.

Children

Hunta is not directed at children and involves hunting-related content. You must be old enough to hold an account under the laws of your country, and in any case at least 16.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. under the GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or erase your personal data. Since all your data is visible and editable inside the app, the fastest route is the app itself; for anything else, email us and we will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the effective date above and note the change in the app or on this page.
