Core features

Built for the serious hunter

Every detail of every hunt, exactly as it happened. No estimates, no fabrication.

Kill Record Cards

Each kill becomes a rich card — animal photo as the cover, species, date, time, and exact GPS coordinates. Organized by country, year, and animal type.

GPX Route + Satellite Map

Import Garmin or Strava GPX files. Your full hunt route renders on a MapLibre satellite view with automatically derived distance and duration.

Weapon & Ammo Logging

Log rifle model, caliber, and bullet grain — or bow type and setup. Ammunition brand and load details. The exact facts that matter for compliance and memory.

Fully Private Portfolio

Your portfolio is yours alone. Firebase Auth + Firestore rules ensure no one else can see your records. Future friend views are read-only projections, never raw access.

Firebase Security Rules Row-level isolation per UID

How it works

Document every
hunt in minutes

Add a kill, the app captures the rest — organized by country, year, and species automatically.

1

Create a kill record

Select species, snap or upload photos, set the kill date. The time is captured automatically when you drop a pin.

2

Log weapon & ammo

Enter rifle model and caliber (or bow type). Add bullet grain and brand. Every field is optional but always exact.

3

Import your GPX route

Drag in a Garmin or Strava file. Distance and duration are derived automatically; the raw GPX is stored verbatim.

4

Browse your portfolio

View kills as a feed or grouped by country. Every card lives at Profile → Country → Year → Animal.
