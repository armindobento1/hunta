A HUNT, PRESERVED

Greater Kudu

Eastern Cape, South Africa · Baviaans Lodge · Tragelaphus strepsiceros
FROM THE JOURNAL

"We began moving before the first light reached the valley. The wind had changed twice before we found the tracks…"

By the time the sun cleared the ridge we had closed to under two hundred metres, the bull quartering away through the spekboom. What Hunta keeps is not the trophy alone — it is the cold of that first hour, the ground we covered, and the people who were there to see it.
